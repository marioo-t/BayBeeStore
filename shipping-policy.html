🚚 Shipping Policy

We’re committed to getting your order to you safely and promptly. Here's how we handle shipping:

⏱️ Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • Orders placed after 5 PM or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.

US Domestic Shipping (United States)

  • Standard delivery: 2–5 business days.
  • Express delivery: 1–2 business days (additional fee applies).
  • Local delivery available in select zones — contact us to confirm coverage.

🌍 International Shipping

  • We currently ship to select countries. Delivery times vary by destination.
  • Customs duties and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

💸 Shipping Costs

  • Calculated at checkout based on weight, destination, and shipping method.
  • Free shipping on orders over [insert amount] — yay!

📦 Order Tracking

  • Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email.
  • Please allow up to 48 hours for tracking updates to appear.

⚠️ Delivery Delays

While we do our best, delays may occur due to weather, customs, or carrier issues. We’ll keep you informed if anything changes.
